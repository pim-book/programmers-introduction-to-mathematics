Infant found dead in car's trunk
Relatives of the car's owner said they were cleaning out the trunk and found the body of an infant, according to Lt. Steve Jones of the Calvert County Sheriff's Office. The car was parked outside a home in Prince Frederick area. Investigators are questioning the vehicle's owner, Jones said. Family members had earlier called police to voice fears about the baby's well-being, CNN affiliate WUSA reported. No charges have been filed yet, the affiliate said.
