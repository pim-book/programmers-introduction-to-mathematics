Actor Michael Madsen arrested
Madsen, 54, was arrested Friday afternoon after deputies were called to his Malibu home, according to a sheriff's department statement. "Madsen, an actor, appeared under the influence of alcohol got into a physical altercation with his teenage son," the statement said The teen did not need medical attention. Madsen, who starred in the movie "Kill Bill," faces a charge of cruelty to a child. Bail was set at $100,000.
